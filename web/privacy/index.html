Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 17, 2026 · Política em português abaixo / Português abajo.

1. Who we are

Oligon Technology (CNPJ 65.292.100/0001-84) is a software engineering company operating the oligontech.com site and shipping SaaS products including Attendari. Our Data Protection Officer (DPO) reaches at privacy@oligontech.com.

2. Scope

This policy covers personal data processed across:

  • oligontech.com — corporate site, inbound leads, consulting clients
  • Attendari — our SaaS product (separate, product-specific addendum at attendari.com/privacy)
  • Future products — each gets its own product-level addendum at launch

3. Data we collect

3.1 Corporate / consulting

  • Name, email, company (from contact forms)
  • Project briefs and technical documents you send us
  • Billing data for invoices (CNPJ/CPF, address)

3.2 SaaS products (Attendari et al.)

  • Account email + subscription data (via Stripe — we don't store card numbers)
  • Business owner data: business name, address, CNPJ, payment-collection keys
  • End-customer data routed through the product (e.g., WhatsApp messages for Attendari)

4. Legal bases (LGPD Art. 7 / GDPR Art. 6)

  • Contract performance — data required to deliver consulting or SaaS
  • Legitimate interest — product improvement, fraud prevention
  • Consent — marketing communications (opt-in)
  • Legal obligation — tax retention, response to authorities

5. Sharing with third parties

We share strictly operational data with vetted sub-processors:

  • Stripe — subscription billing
  • Mercado Pago — Pix charge collection (Attendari)
  • Anthropic PBC — LLM inference (Claude API)
  • Meta Platforms — WhatsApp Business API (Attendari)
  • Cloudflare — hosting, database, edge processing
  • Resend — transactional email
  • Google Cloud — Calendar integration (opt-in)

We do not sell personal data. We do not use one customer's data to train models shared across customers.

6. Retention

  • Account data: while the subscription is active + 6 months
  • Product data (Attendari WhatsApp messages, etc.): per product policy (typically 12 months)
  • Technical logs: 90 days
  • Tax-related data: 5 years (legal requirement)

7. Your rights (LGPD Art. 18)

You may, at any time, request: confirmation of processing; access; correction; anonymization, blocking, or deletion; portability; revocation of consent; information about sharing.

To exercise any right, write to privacy@oligontech.com. We respond within 15 days.

8. Security

  • Encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest
  • Audit logs for sensitive operations
  • Daily backups with 30-day retention
  • Principle of least privilege for engineering access

9. Cookies

oligontech.com: language preference stored in localStorage. No tracking, no advertising, no third-party cookies.

Attendari dashboard: session cookie (HttpOnly, Secure, SameSite=Lax) issued by us. No third-party tracking.

10. Changes

Material changes are communicated 30 days in advance via email to active customers.

11. Contact

Questions, requests, exercise of rights: privacy@oligontech.com.
